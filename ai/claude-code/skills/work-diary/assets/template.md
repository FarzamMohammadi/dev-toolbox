# Entry Templates

These are starting points, not rules. Rename sections, add new ones, drop what doesn't fit.

---

## For a shipped project, feature, or substantial piece of work:

```markdown
---

## [YYYY-MM-DD] - [Brief Title]

### Intention
What I set out to build and why it mattered.

### The Journey
What actually happened — the problem, the approach, what broke, what worked.
The story of getting from A to B, including the wrong turns.

### Key Decisions
The choices that shaped the outcome and why I made them.

### What I Learned
Insights, patterns, skills — things I'll carry forward.

### Reflections
How I feel about it, what I'd do differently, what's next.
```

---

## For an insight, mistake, or career moment:

```markdown
---

## [YYYY-MM-DD] - [Brief Title]

### Context
What happened.

### Insight
What I learned from it.

### Going Forward
What I'll do differently.
```
